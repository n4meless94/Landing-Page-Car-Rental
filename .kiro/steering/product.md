# Product Overview

## Eean Enterprise Car Rental

A modern car rental booking platform for Kota Kinabalu, Malaysia. The product enables customers to quickly browse vehicle rates and book rentals via WhatsApp integration.

### Key Features
- **WhatsApp Booking**: Primary booking flow through WhatsApp with pre-filled messages
- **Rate Calculator**: Dynamic pricing estimation for daily and 3+ day packages
- **Vehicle Catalog**: 13 different car models from economy to luxury (Proton Saga to Toyota Alphard)
- **Responsive Design**: Mobile-first approach with dark theme
- **Contact Integration**: Direct phone and email contact options

### Target Users
- Tourists visiting Kota Kinabalu
- Local residents needing temporary vehicle rentals
- Business travelers requiring reliable transportation

### Business Model
- Car rental service with transparent pricing
- WhatsApp-first customer communication
- Local Sabah market focus with multiple vehicle categories
