Frequently Asked

Quick answers to common questions

Is availability guaranteed?

Availability depends on dates and current bookings. WhatsApp us to confirm your preferred vehicle and dates.

How does the 3+ day package work?

Book for 3 or more days to get our discounted package rate. Additional days beyond 3 are charged at the daily rate.

Where can I pick up the car?

We offer flexible pickup at Kota Kinabalu Airport, hotels, or other locations in the city. Just let us know your preference.

What documents do I need?

You'll need a valid driving license and identification. International visitors should have an International Driving Permit.

Is there a deposit required?

Yes, a refundable deposit is required. The amount varies by vehicle type. We'll confirm the details when you book.
